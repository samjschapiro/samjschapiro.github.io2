Samuel Schapiro

My name is Sam. I am studying the science of intelligence using mathematics, computer science, philosophy, and psychology

I am currently the founding research scientist of a multidisciplinary AI research lab called Spiral Works, working on computational creativity and other understudied aspects of intelligence. Previously, I was a visiting researcher at the Simons Institute for the Theory of Computing, UC Berkeley studying Modern Paradigms in Generalization.

During my undergrad in CS at UIUC, I have been fortunate to study transfer learning and deep learning theory with the brilliant Han Zhao and the glorious Arindam Banerjee. I like to read and write.
